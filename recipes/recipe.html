Pork Chops and Rice

Description

Brown some chops, then add vegetables, broth and rice. This dinner only needs one skillet, a knife, a cuttin board, some measureing cups and spoons a can and a mixing and serving spoon. We like this with simple quesadillas and a salad.

ingredients

  • 2 tablespoon olive oil
  • 4 boneless pork chops,3/4 inch thick
  • 2 teaspoon taco seosoning spice blend
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup chopped onion
  • 1 cup coarsely chopped green bell pepper
  • 2 clove galrlic,minced
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder

Steps to Prepare

  1. Season both sides of pork chops with taco
    seasoning, cumin, smoked prapika, and salt
  2. Place chops in the hot skillet and cook, turning
    once, until browned on both sides, about 2 minutes
    per side. Remove to a plate and keep warm.
  3. cook and stir onion and bell pepper, until the
    vegetable just begin to pick up a little color, about 2
    mintes. Add garlic and cook until fragrant, about 30 seconds
  4. Pour in diced tomatoes and green chillies, with their juice, and stir,
    being sure to scrape up any browned bits on the bottom of the skillet.
  5. Add chili powder and black pepper to the chicken broth, and stir in.
  6. Add thawed corn kernels, sliced zicchini, and uncooked rice.
    Stir until vegetable are evenly distributed, and make sure all
    the rice is submerged in the cooking liquid. Bring to a boil
